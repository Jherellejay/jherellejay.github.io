How do I book in for a tattoo

To begin the process of booking your tattoo, please use the enquiry form. Make sure you include size & placement as a minimum, as well as anything else you feel would be helpful i.e. reference images & budget. Once we have discussed your ideas, I will either ask you to come in for a face to face consultation or you can book straight in via the studio directly. You can only do this once I've given you a price & time quote.

Do I need to pay a deposit?

All booking require a minimum deposit of £50, ideally to be paid in cash in the studio, alternatively we can accept PayPal payment. All deposits are non-refundable.

How much do you charge?

My current prices are £60ph or £250 per day, a full day sitting is 11am-4:30pm with a 30 minute lunch break.

Do I need to have a consultation?

Face-to-face consultations are te best way to book a tattoo. It makes it easier for me to understand what eactly you are looking for and how I can achieve that. Although I understand these may not always be possible which is why I have an enquiry form and can consult with you via email.

Will I see my design before the day of my tattoo?

Generally i will book enouugh time in your appointment toallow for any change to be made. As my diary is busy I can only draw designs a day or two in advance and may not have the time to do further consultations.

What happens if I need to cancel/reschedule my appointment?

If you need to cancel your tattoo entirely please be considerate and try to give at least 48 hours notice. The same applies for re-scheduling. Deposits can only be carried over if the correct notice period has been given. Deposits will not be refunded for cancelled appintments.
